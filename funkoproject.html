The Art of Funko Pop!

The Magic of Pops

What is Funko? Comparable to figurines, Funko Pop vinyl figures are based on icons, popular culture, comic books, video games, and movies.
These toys with nostalgic themes come with gigantic heads and eyes, portraying well-known licensed characters from many franchises in a variety of sizes, clothing, stances, and finishes.
In addition, Funko Pop merchandise is packaged in a stylish display box, which makes it the perfect collection for fans of pop culture enthusiasts, from movies to icons. Therefore, Funko's Pop range provides something for everyone, regardless of their interests!

Value of Collections

Collecting Funko Pops can generate value over time which is why it is a very popular hobby. Funkos when they first vocme out can range from $13 to $70 and can possible stay at that value, however with time, demand, and rarity; the value can jump. The rarest Pop can be valued around $10k! There are some that reach over $100k, however you don't see them as often. Nonetheless, Pops' worth fluctuates from time to time, thus collecting the rarest design has a higher chance of appreciating in value over time.
